Privacy Policy

Effective July 18, 2026

Enlightiam Creator (“Enlightiam,” “we,” “us”) helps creators review photo carousels and transfer approved content to TikTok for further editing and publishing. This policy explains what information we process and the choices available to you.

Information we process

  • TikTok authorization data: TikTok open ID, granted scopes, access token, refresh token, and token expiration information. Tokens are encrypted before storage and are never placed in browser-visible code.
  • Creator content: the images, titles, descriptions, and hashtags you choose to upload or transfer.
  • Technical data: limited request, error, and security information needed to operate and protect the service.

How we use information

We use this information to connect the TikTok account you authorize, host your selected images long enough for TikTok or Buffer to retrieve them, initiate transfers you explicitly approve, maintain security, and troubleshoot the service. We do not sell personal information or use TikTok authorization data for advertising.

Sharing

We send approved carousel content and related metadata to the service you choose when you approve a transfer. We also use infrastructure providers to host the application and content. These providers process information only to provide their services to us or as required by law.

Retention and deletion

TikTok authorization records are retained while your account remains connected. Uploaded carousel files and their public transfer links expire automatically seven days after upload. A daily cleanup removes expired files. Some limited security records may be retained where reasonably necessary to prevent abuse or comply with law.

Your choices

You can review and change the content before transfer, decline a transfer, disconnect TikTok from the creator workspace, or revoke Enlightiam in TikTok’s app-permissions settings. You can request access or deletion by emailing privacy@enlightiam.com.

Security

We use encrypted transport, server-side credential handling, encrypted token storage, restricted scopes, anti-forgery checks, and access controls designed to protect information. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

Children

Enlightiam is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us so we can investigate and delete it where appropriate.

Changes and contact

We may update this policy and will change the effective date above. Questions may be sent to privacy@enlightiam.com.
